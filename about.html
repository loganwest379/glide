Meet Logan.

That's me in the middle. The one with the fiery red helmet. My parents got me and my siblings all started skiing when we were 3 years old and I have been ever since. Whether visiting our local ski resort on the first snowfall of the season or learning to wax skis with my dad in the garage every year, skiing has been a big part of my life. I love everything about skiing and I started Glide to deepen my learning and way to serve the community.

My Home Mountain

My roots take me to the Wenatchee Valley where I grew up skiing at Mission Ridge. This gem of a resort in Washington state truly taught me everything I know about skiing – from navigating the chutes in the Bomber Cliffs to finding open bowls out back. Learning to wax skis with my dad in the garage was just another part of growing up surrounded by the sport.

BYU & Beyond

When I'm not on skis, I'm studying Strategic Management at BYU, aiming for a career in product management. I enjoy the challenge of analyzing complex problems and figuring out what makes a successful product or service connect with people.

Life's Adventures

The best thing to ever happen to me was marrying my wife, Natalie. She grew up in California, and now we love exploring together – everything from the mountains nearby to trips to the beach. It's amazing having a partner to share adventures with, and gaining a new ski buddy is definitely a bonus!
