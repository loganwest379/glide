Meet Logan.

That's me in the middle. The one with the fiery red helmet. My parents got me and my siblings all started skiing when we were 3 years old and I have been ever since. Whether visiting our local ski resort on the first snowfall of the season or learning to wax skis with my dad in the garage every year, skiing has been a big part of my life. I love everything about skiing and I started Glide to deepen my learning and way to serve the community.
